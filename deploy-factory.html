⚙️ OPNet Factory Deployer

Deploy and initialize your OP-20 token factory in 4 steps.

1
Connect OPWallet
Connect your testnet OPWallet to sign transactions.
2
Deploy Token Template
Upload build/MyToken.wasm and deploy it as the base token template.
3
Deploy Factory
Upload build/MyFactory.wasm and deploy the factory contract.
4
Initialize Factory
Link the token template and fee recipient to the factory. Required before anyone can create tokens.
